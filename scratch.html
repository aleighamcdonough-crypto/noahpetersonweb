Entrepreneurship Project

Business Plan

Helping local business improve their online visibility and generate more leads. My plan is to begin with a real business I already have access to so I can test strategies, track results, and prove what works before expanding. I want to focus on improving online presence, attracting more customers, and measuring real outcomes like calls and leads. As I gain experience, I will refine my process and use those results to grow into a long-term business.

Why I Want to Start My Own Business

I want to start my own business so I can build something that is truly mine and create real value for others. I like the idea of being in control of my work, making important decisions, and seeing the direct results of my effort. I also want the opportunity to grow financially while helping businesses solve problems, especially by improving their visibility and bringing them more opportunities. Starting my own business will allow me to learn, take risks, and create something that can continue growing over time.

Important Skills for Starting a Business

  1. Sales – getting clients and confidently closing deals.
  2. Communication – clearly explaining your value and building trust.
  3. Discipline – staying consistent even when results are not immediate.
  4. Marketing – attracting leads and getting attention online.
  5. Problem-solving – figuring things out and adapting when needed.
  6. Time management – focusing on the tasks that actually grow the business.
